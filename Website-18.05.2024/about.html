ABOUT

The Centre has been addressing the natural resource survey, monitoring, and planning needs of the State for over four decades. It actively participates in Department of Space collaborative projects and regularly assists state user departments in planning development activities using Remote Sensing and GIS techniques. The Centre has created various thematic and administrative layers at different scales and time periods, serving as the foundation for numerous application projects. Notable achievements include the Resource Atlas of Rajasthan, Watershed Atlas of Rajasthan, Ground Water Atlas of Rajasthan, Panchayat Samiti-wise Master Plan of Water Harvesting Structure sites, silt prevention studies in Pushkar Sarovar, Conservation Planning of Jodhpur Lift Canal, Wetland Mapping, Wasteland Mapping, among others. The Centre is equipped with the latest infrastructure facilities for Remote Sensing and GIS-based studies.

VISION

Moving from information support towards decision support for sustainable development & management of natural resources along with infrastructure development and urban planning through the application of remote sensing and GIS technology.

PROJECT CATEGORIES

Generating numerous geospatial layers for information support, Building natural resources information system, Land-use & land-cover (LULC) mapping, crop acreage estimation, surficial waterbodies mapping, groundwater quality mapping, wasteland mapping, land-degradation mapping, wetland mapping, forest blocks mapping, geomorphological & lineaments mapping, infrastructural mapping, cadastral mapping, administrative boundaries demarcation

Planning various natural resources management and development planning activities, Supporting decentralized rural development planning, forecasting agricultural outputs, Panchayat Samiti wise master-plan of water-harvesting-structure sites, silt prevention studies in Pushkar Lake, conservation planning of Jodhpur lift canal

Monitoring the landscape through change detection activities, Watersheds monitoring, waterbodies monitoring, river-flow change analysis, monitoring the crop-residue-burning, eco-sensitive zone monitoring, LULC change detection around mining areas, wasteland change analysis, forest cover change analysis, encroachment studies, industrial green cover assessment, Capacity building of the user departments / students / common man, Organizing trainings/courses/workshops/seminars, preparing atlases such as Resource Atlas of Rajasthan, Ground Water Atlas of Rajasthan, Watershed Atlas of Rajasthan, Ayurvedic Medicinal Plants Atlas of Rajasthan, Soil Resource Atlas or Rajasthan, exclusive workshops for spatial empowerment of Panchayati Raj Institutions.
